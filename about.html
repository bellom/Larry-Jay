About

Hi, I'm HON Lanre Jaji! a man of the people that believes in good governance and to serve and make life comfortable for the public.

I went to L.A primary school in Ilaro,Luba nursery/primary school in Ijebu ode, worked briefly as a court clerk in HIGH COURT OF JUSTICE Isabo, Abeokuta. I then proceeded to Ogun State College Of Health Technology to attain my Diploma in Pharmacy. Then proceeded to UNILAG to read Bsc in Chemistry and later to LASU to read M.sc in Analytical and Environmental Chemisty in view. Proceeded to UNESCO Institute for water education to study water treatment processes and plants in amsterdam, Netherland and now a consultant on water treatment plant design. Contested the 2015 election under PDP for Federal house of Rep for Odeda/Obafemi Owode/Abeokuta North federal constituency.
